This clock uses a spiral, where the distance from the center corresponds with the minimum and maximum values for the time.
Additionally, the saturation is ordered from the smallest unit (seconds) having the lowest saturation to the largest unit (hours) having the largest saturation.
The angles also match those of a clock face to help with telling the time to the closest quarter.
